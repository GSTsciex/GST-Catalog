Singapore

Sales Office SCIEX (Distribution) and Training Centre

11, Biopolis Way, #08-08, Helios
Singapore 138667, Tel: 65594338

On the first day of training, head to the sales office (at Chromos building, #03-06) first at 9am. Look for Jackie, Joey, Caslyn to collect an access pass (blue) for the training center. At Chromos level 1 lobby, you need to get a security pass first before going upstairs to our office. Once you have the blue temporary access pass, it allows you to enter the training center. You do not need the security pass anymore and can return it back to the security counter at the lobby.

Singapore is warm about 30 degrees Celsius all year round. Please wear a comfortable shirt/collar T-shirt and slacks will do. Slippers and shorts are not allowed in the training center.

Transportation & Directions

From city area hotel to the training centre

Singapore is a small city and transportation is convenient by taxi or MRT.

1) Taxi: It will cost about S$15 to $25 (surcharges included) from the city area to training centre. Takes about 20-30 min. Mention to the driver that our office is in 'Biopolis', located near 'Ministry of Education' or near 'Buona Vista'

2) MRT (local) Subway: There is a subway station located near the training centre. Station name is Buona Vista. Once you exit the station, walk towards Biopolis, it is about 5 to 10 minutes. This is very convenient and will take 30 to 40 mines from city area.

From airport to hotel

Prepared to pay about S$15 to $25. (1 US$ about = S$1.40) for the trip from the airport to your hotel. In additional to meter fare, there are additional surcharge of $3.50 from the airport and other surcharge. Some taxis do not accept credit card therefore have some Singapore currency at hand. Get a receipt from the driver.

Hotel

Training Centre is located within 20-30min from all hotels in Singapore. Please look for Jackie (Service and Training Admin) for assistance in booking of hotel. You should receive your hotel confirmation by now.
